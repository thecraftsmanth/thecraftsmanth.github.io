F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What subjects are taught here?

    We focus on students skill development such as speaking skills, reading skills, wrriting skills, basic grammer practice.

  • How many days we oparate in a week?

    In general, we require and recommend our students 3 days per week but it is adjustable according to students need.

  • How much do you take as payment?

    Usually we take 800 BDT per month from a student, there is almost always discount and benefits for meritorious students.

  • How do you accept payments?

    We accept Cash, bKash, Rocket, Nagad and Bank Transfers.

  • Do you take exams?

    Yes, We do take exams. There is no easy way for students to measure their learning progress excepts exam, so we take regular model test to make sure our students are learning.

  • How do you publish results?

    As we regularly take exams we have to make sure the privacy of a students results are maintained. We publish result secretly only the student and his or her parents can know about their result.
